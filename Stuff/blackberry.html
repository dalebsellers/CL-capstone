We were very pleased with the staff and all there is to do at Spring Mill Park! Delightful location, lots of trails for walking, excellent staff, nice room and a great restaurant. If you are looking for a family friendly break from the daily grind - this is a good choice.

I really enjoyed this park. The inn is very nice, great service from the front desk. The hiking was great. The trails are fun and most are easy enough for a part time hiker like me. There’s an indoor pool at the inn along with a few arcade type games. Really nice!
